Changes & Fixes

Issues identified from Round 1 and improvements implemented for Round 2

Concerns from Round 1
IMD 39.6% missing — Label parsing broke as.integer(). Only quintiles 2–4 visible. Fixed
Immunosuppression (medication) = 0% — DMD codes queried against SNOMED field. Removed
Registration fixed to 2017-01-01 — Late-arriving patients excluded incorrectly. Fixed
Antibiotic window overlap — Prior-month & prior-year overlapped; index date included. Redesigned
AMR ±30d window too wide — May capture unrelated episodes. ±14d
Missing risk factors vs CPRD — 27 of 60 conditions from van Staa 2024. 39 now
No primary care BSI — Community-managed episodes missed. Added
No incidence rates — Raw counts, no denominators. Measures added
No control cohort — Cannot study risk for BSI. Created
Implemented for Round 2
IMD → built-in .imd_quintile — Integer 1–5, no label parsing.
Registration → rolling — ≥1yr before each patient's index date.
4 antibiotic variables — Non-overlapping windows: abx_6w, abx_prior_year_cat, abx_classes_6w, abx_classes_prior_year. Index date excluded.
AMR window ±14 days — Matches culture turnaround + GP coding delay.
+12 comorbidities — Frailty (eFI), housebound, PVD, VTE, organ transplant, IBD, RA, Parkinson's, HIV, osteoporosis, hypothyroidism.
12 antibiotic class sub-codelists — 3,663 DMD codes classified into penicillins, cephalosporins, macrolides, etc.
Combined dataset — ICD-10 + SNOMED primary care BSI with source classification.
Measures framework — 7 monthly rate measures (overall, resistant, by IMD/region/age/sex). 108 intervals.
Control cohort — No-BSI population pool with 39 comorbidities for 1:6 osmatching.
